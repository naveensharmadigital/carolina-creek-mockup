Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Mooresville, NC?
Most bathroom remodels with Carolina Creek range from $5,000 to $18,000 depending on scope, materials, and size. We provide a transparent, itemized quote during your free in-home consultation — no hidden fees, no surprises. Financing is available to fit any budget.
How long does a bathroom remodel take?
Most of our projects are completed in 1–5 days. A tub-to-shower conversion is typically done in 1–2 days. A full bathroom remodel usually takes 3–5 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
Do you use real stone or acrylic panels?
We use premium real stone panels — never plastic, acrylic, or composite shortcuts. Our groutless stone panels are mold-resistant, crack-resistant, and designed to look stunning for decades without intensive maintenance.
Do I need to move out during the remodel?
In most cases, no. Our team works efficiently and keeps your home protected. If you have a second bathroom, it will remain fully accessible throughout the project. We’ll discuss everything upfront so there are no surprises.
Is Carolina Creek licensed and insured?
Yes. Carolina Creek Tub & Shower, LLC is fully licensed and insured in both North Carolina and South Carolina. We’re also BBB Accredited and hold a 5-star Google rating from 120+ verified reviews.
What does your lifetime warranty cover?
Our lifetime warranty covers craftsmanship defects, material failures, and installation issues for the life of your home. If anything isn’t right, we return and fix it — no charge, no questions asked. Payment isn’t even due until you’re 100% satisfied.
Do you serve areas outside Mooresville?
Yes! We serve the entire Greater Charlotte area including Huntersville, Cornelius, Davidson, Concord, Charlotte, Matthews, Statesville, Greensboro, Winston-Salem, and across the border into Fort Mill, Rock Hill, York, and surrounding SC communities.
